Pasta Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara, one of the most famous Pasta Recipes of Roman Cuisine, is made only with 5 simple ingredients.

DO NOT USE: garlic, parsley, onion, cream, milk, parmigiano or bacon.

Ingredients for a classic carbonara:

  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Eggs
  • Pepper
  • Spaghetti
